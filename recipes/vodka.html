Vodka Soda Recipe

Description

A vodka soda is a simple, refreshing drink made with just two ingredients: vodka and soda water. It's known for its clean taste and low-calorie content.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Soda water

Steps

  1. Chill: Fill a highball glass with ice and let it sit for a minute to chill the glass. Discard the melted ice water.
  2. Measure: Pour your desired amount of vodka into the chilled glass. A typical starting point is 1.5 ounces, but you can adjust to your taste.
  3. Top with soda Top the glass with chilled soda water, leaving a little room at the top.
    4. Garnish (optional) Add a squeeze of fresh lime or lemon for a touch of citrus flavor.
  4. Stir gently and enjoy!
