Caipirinha Recipe

Description

The Caipirinha (pronounced kai-pee-REEN-yah) is Brazil's refreshing national cocktail. It's a simple and delicious drink made with just a few ingredients: cachaça (Brazilian rum), limes, and sugar.

The limes and sugar are muddled together to release their flavors, then the cachaça and ice are added for a boozy, citrusy treat. It's perfect for summertime or anytime you need a taste of the tropics.

Ingredients

  • 2 limes, wedged
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 oz cachaça (Brazilian rum)
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Muddle lime wedges and sugar in a glass.
  2. Fill with ice, add cachaça, and stir.
  3. Garnish with a lime wedge. Enjoy!
