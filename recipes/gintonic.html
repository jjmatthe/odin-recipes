Gin & Tonic Recipe

Description

A gin and tonic is a classic highball cocktail featuring gin, tonic water, and a citrus garnish, typically lime.

It's known for its refreshing taste with a balance of juniper (from the gin) and bitter citrus notes (from the tonic). It's a simple yet versatile drink, and the ratio of gin to tonic can be adjusted to your preference.

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Tonic Water
  • Garnish (Optional)

Steps

  • Fill a highball glass with ice.
  • Pour in your desired amount of gin (typically 1-2 ounces).
  • Top with chilled tonic water, leaving space at the rim.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge (optional) and enjoy!
